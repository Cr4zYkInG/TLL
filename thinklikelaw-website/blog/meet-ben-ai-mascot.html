Meet Ben: The AI Mascot Redefining How Law Students Study

By Editorial Team | 6 min read | Feb 20, 2026

Law school is famously isolating. Long nights in the library, complex case law, and the constant pressure of academic rigour can weigh on even the most dedicated students. At ThinkLikeLaw, we believe technology should do more than just process data—it should offer companionship, encouragement, and real-time assistance.

Enter Ben: the world's first interactive AI Legal Mascot. He's not just a cute icon in the corner; he's a sophisticated, state-aware study buddy built to make your academic journey smoother, smarter, and arguably a lot more fun.

More Than a Mascot: A Desktop Companion

Ben was designed with a "stealth-first" philosophy. He understands that when you’re in deep focus, you don't want distractions. That’s why Ben stays out of sight until you need him. Whether it’s through the new white cat head icon in your navigation bar or the Shift+B keyboard shortcut, summoning Ben is an event in itself—featuring a cinematic "Grand Entrance" that isolates your workstation for a moment of zen before you dive into the books.

Pro Tip

Ben’s "Grand Entrance" is strictly on a 3-minute cooldown to prevent distraction. Use it when you need a mental reset between long reading sessions.

Real-Time Legal Spelling & Grammar

We’ve all been there: typing at 2 AM and losing track of legal terminology. Is it judgement or judgment? Liabal or liable? Ben has a built-in legal-aware spellcheck engine. But he doesn't just underline your mistakes.

When Ben detects a typo in the ThinkLikeLaw Note Editor, he will physically move from the corner of your screen to the exact location of the misspelled word. He’ll sit just below the line, speak to you through a non-intrusive bubble, and offer the correction. The best part? Simply click Ben, and he’ll swap the word for you instantly, maintaining your flow and formatting.

The Interactive Legal Thesaurus

Finding the right academic tone is one of the hardest parts of law essay writing. Ben solves this with his Interactive Thesaurus. By double-clicking Ben, you activate "Study Assistant Mode." Highlight any word in your notes, and Ben uses his advanced AI core to provide 3-4 sophisticated legal synonyms or a concise definition.

Whether you need to swap "agreement" for "covenant" or better understand a specific term of art, Ben is there. This feature is powered by the same high-end AI that drives our Essay Marker and Case Summariser, ensuring your vocabulary is always partner-level.

A Focus-First Audio Engine

Ben isn't just a visual aid; he’s an auditory one too. In our latest update, we’ve integrated the Audio & Focus Suite. Ben now has a voice—gentle meows when he arrives and a satisfied purr when he enters a 'happy' state. More importantly, Ben acts as your DJ.

Through the new music icon in the Note Editor, you can have Ben play loopable, focus-enhancing tracks:

  • Lo-Fi Beats: For high-pressure drafting.
  • Deep Brown Noise: For total library immersion.
  • Rainy Library: For that classic academic vibe.

True to our commitment to productivity, all of Ben’s audio features are muted by default. You have total control via the Settings > Preferences panel, allowing you to tailor Ben’s presence to your specific study needs.

The Future of Legal Tech is Personal

At the end of the day, Ben represents our vision for the future of legal technology: tools that are as supportive as they are powerful. He’s a reminder that while law is a serious business, studying it doesn't have to be a lonely one.

Log in today, press Shift+B, and meet your new favorite study buddy. Ben is ready to help you master the Law, one word at a time.
